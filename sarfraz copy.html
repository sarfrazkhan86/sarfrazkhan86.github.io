Career Goals

My current career path was chosen based on job security and my high aptitude for numbers. Although I am content with what I do, I'd like to be more than just content. I've always had an interest in technology ever since my parents got me my first computer as a teenager. That interest never faded away.

Recently my thought process has changed regarding making a career choice. I believe that the number one priority when seeking a career is making sure you will enjoy your work. You dont have to necessarily choose a career path based on only what you are good at. Doing work that you enjoy day in and day out will have a positive impact on your work ethic. The increase in work ethic will in turn make you better at what you do. So, I started to code.

These days I am spending any and all free time I have towards learning to code. I have been taking online classes on HTML, CSS, and Javascript. Thus far the journey has been fun and challenging to say the least. I've set a timeline for myself to break into the Web Design industry and hopefully if all goes well I will have the skills needed to do so within the next six months.
